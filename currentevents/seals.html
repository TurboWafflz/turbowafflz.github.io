Seals getting eels stuck in their noses

Recently, scientists have found several Hawaiian Monk seals with eels stuck in their noses. They don't know why it is happening, but they have several theories:

  1. The eel swam in to try to avoid being eaten
  2. They are regurgitated out of the nose
  3. The seals put them there on purpose
Surprisingly, the scientists think that #3 is the most plausible as the seals' nostrils close when they are swimming, and the eels are too big to be regurgitated and come out of their noses. Luckily, all of the seals have been healthy after having the eels removed.
