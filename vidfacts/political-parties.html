10 Facts from the video "Political Parties"

  1. Candidates for an election are selected in primaries
  2. Most states require you to be a registered party member to vote in primaries
  3. If you do not want to run with a party, you can run as an independent
  4. Parties recruit candidates
  5. Parties nominate candidates
  6. Parties influence national government
  7. States that no not require party registration to vote in primaries, have problems with people voting for a candidate in the other party that they know will not win, so their party wins
  8. Party conventions are not really functional anymore
  9. Only 25% of people who can vote in primaries do
  10. People who do vote in primaries tend to have extreme views
