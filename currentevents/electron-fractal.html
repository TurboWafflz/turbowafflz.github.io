1.58 Dimensional Electron Fractal

Scientists have created the 1.58 dimensional Sierpinsky triangle out of electrons. They placed carbon monoxide molecules on a copper surface so that electrons in the copper make somthing close to a fractal known as a Sierpinsky triangle. A complete Sierpinsky triangle however would repeat infinitely, so the scientists' fractal is only an approximation of it with only a few repititions.
